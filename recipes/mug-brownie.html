Mug Brownie

Easy+Microwave+Brownie=Lazy Dessert

Let's picture a scenario: You have only 2 minutes to cook something and you crave something sweet. You only have a few ingredients and a microwave. Then why don't you try a delicious and easy mug brownie? It is foolproof and really only takes 2 minutes. One for mixing the ingredients and one inside the microwave. It might sound weird that you can cook a thing like this in the microwave but it can be done, perfectly, it won't come out uncooked. But brownies are meant to "look not completely cooked" so keep that in mind.

You will need 9 ingredients, most likely you have at least 7 or 8. If you don't you can buy them anywhere and, since you only use a tablespoon of each, it will last a long time. Which means brownies for a long time. Psycology says that chocolate tastes better in orange mugs, so maybe you might need to buy one. I cook mine in my old, stained, a bit broken, white Breaking Bad mug. The logo is almost gone and the brownies still taste really good. It's about chemistry, not about your feelings. It is sacred. You must respect the quemistry Jesse! So if you don't have an orange mug it's ok.

  • 3 Tbsp of flour
  • 2 Tbsp of sugar
  • 2 Tbsp of cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Drop of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 Tbsp of baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp of butter or cooking oil
  • 3 Tbsp of milk
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine all the dry ingredients in the mug.
  2. Then add all the wet ingredients. The vanilla is optional.
  3. Place the chocolate chips on top at the end.
  4. Put it in the microwave for a minute.
  5. Be careful, is it hot. Enjoy.
